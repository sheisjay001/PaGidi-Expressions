Who is PaGidi?

PaGidi is all about giving humans the privilege and opportunity to speak their minds, express themselves, be listened to, and be understood. Not just through written and spoken media, but also in person.

With a straight-from-the-heart, non-conformist, direct, and blunt approach to controversial issues, PaGidi uses a unique writing style and relatable stories. This approach helps people shed mental weights, emotional aches, and disturbing problems in ways that become life-changing experiences.

A self-professed non-practicing Christian, PaGidi has been featured on television shows including TVC Wake Up Nigeria and Breakfast in Bed on Lagos Television.
